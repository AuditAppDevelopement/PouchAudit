Privacy Policy

Pouch Audit  ·  Last updated: 2 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how Sean Fernyhough ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use Pouch Audit (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously and aim to be transparent about what data we collect, how we use it, and with whom it may be shared.

1. Information We Collect

Pouch Audit collects only the information you directly provide within the app. This includes:

Usage data — pouch entries you log, including timestamps, brand names, nicotine strength, and cost per pouch.

Preferences — settings such as your chosen currency, daily limit, and brand library.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal identifying information unless you choose to contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data you enter is used solely to provide the features of the app — tracking your usage, displaying statistics, and calculating cost projections. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond the functionality of the app.

3. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. Third-Party Services

We currently do not share your data with any third parties. However, we reserve the right to integrate third-party services in future versions of the app — for example, analytics tools or crash reporting services. If we do so, this policy will be updated and you will be informed. Any third-party services we use will be required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device and, if iCloud is enabled, in your iCloud account. You can delete your data at any time by deleting entries within the app or by uninstalling the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Pouch Audit is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since all data is stored locally on your device or in your own iCloud account, you are in full control of your data at all times. To exercise any rights or raise concerns, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
